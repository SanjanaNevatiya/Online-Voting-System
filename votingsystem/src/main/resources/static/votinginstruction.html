How to Vote

Follow these simple steps to cast your vote:

  1. Ensure you are registered to vote. Check your registration status on the official election website.
  2. Find your designated polling station or check if you can vote by mail.
  3. Gather necessary identification documents if required by your local election authority.
  4. Visit your polling station or follow the instructions for mail-in voting.
  5. Follow the instructions provided at the polling station or on the mail-in ballot.
  6. Submit your completed ballot according to the instructions.
  7. Confirm that your vote has been counted. Some places offer online tracking of your ballot.
